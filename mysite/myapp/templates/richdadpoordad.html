Rich Dad Poor Dad is a treasure trove of financial wisdom. In the book Robert Kyosaki discusses
the lessons that he learned growing up from his "Rich dad" and how he applied those lessons to
earn a fortune. These are the lessons that the wealthy teach their children. The book lightly
discusses topics such as real estate investing, small cap stocks, tax leins and more, but none
of these are covered in much detail (the author does recommend numerous other books that cover
these topics in more details).
Overall Rich Dad Poor Dad is an easy read with strong financial philosophies that anyone can follow
to acquire wealth.

Rating: 9.5/10
